## Character sprites

The nine 80×80 transparent PNGs in `assets/trainers/` come from the [Pokémon Showdown trainer sprite index](https://play.pokemonshowdown.com/sprites/trainers/). The index warns that many entries are community-made, requires the appropriate artist credit where shown, and says not to edit sprites without permission. These files are stored byte-for-byte as downloaded and are resized only through CSS.

## Trainer card reference

`assets/trainers/trainer-card-frlg.png` is the 225×300 PNG thumbnail of `Trainer Card FRLG 1Star Male.png` from the [Bulbagarden Trainer Card gallery](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Trainer%27s_card), SHA-256 `63F0F0BD930A786BA06BC6184B4CCF1E51261738A447E3D4714060EBC6E404CD`. It is used as a faint, unedited visual texture behind the guide's dynamic Trainer Card content; the selected character sprite and profile text remain separate HTML elements.
