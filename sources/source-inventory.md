## Known conflicts and gaps

- The official forum opening post says 553 visible Pokédex entries plus Form Changer acquisitions, while the newer community workbook says 596 Pokémon. Treat the workbook as the stronger roster lead but verify forms and exact availability against the 2026-07-19 build.
- Standard workbook encounters are treated as all-day. Do not invent morning/day/night splits.
- Seasonal Migration is a separate overlay. The workbook identifies seasonal species pools by region, and the developer changelog corrects July summer entries from 100% to 1%; it does not narrow the region-wide pools to individual routes.
- Searches across the workbook found no references to One through Six Island, Kindle Road, Ember Cavern, Crystal Cavern, Memorial Pass/Pillar, Sevii Waterway/Ruins, Resort Gorgeous, Ruins Valley/Cavern or Berry Forest. Structured post-2026-07-01 Sevii data remains required.
