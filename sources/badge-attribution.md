# Badge tracker sources and artwork

## Badge identities

The user supplied the Pokémon Crystal Advance Redux badge-item list on 2026-07-22. The tracker preserves its consecutive memory identifiers in `config/game-config.json`:

- Johto: `82003884 018B` through `82003884 0192`.
- Kanto: `82003884 0193` through `82003884 019A`.

The supplied labels `Eceuteak Badge` and `Celedon Badge` are displayed as the canonical city spellings `Ecruteak Badge` and `Celadon Badge`. The game-specific memory identifiers are retained for provenance but are not presented as cheat instructions in the Overview interface.

## Artwork

The 16 badge PNGs under `assets/art/badges/` were copied from the local Pokémon Heart & Soul Guide maintained in the same workspace. Their longer source filenames credit DeviantArt creator **JormxDos**; this guide uses the corresponding short filenames. The artwork depicts the standard Johto and Kanto badge designs and is mapped to the city-labelled Crystal Advance Redux entries as follows:

- Violet/Zephyr, Azalea/Hive, Goldenrod/Plain, Ecruteak/Fog, Cianwood/Storm, Olivine/Mineral, Mahogany/Glacier and Blackthorn/Rising.
- Pewter/Boulder, Cerulean/Cascade, Vermilion/Thunder, Celadon/Rainbow, Fuchsia/Soul, Saffron/Marsh, Cinnabar/Volcano and Viridian/Earth.

No explicit redistribution licence was located in the local source material. Creator credit is preserved here, and the underlying Pokémon badge designs remain the property of their respective rights holders.
